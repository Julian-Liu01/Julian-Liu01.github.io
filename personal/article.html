Article:Meditation and Mental Health

English version:

Mental illness has became more and more common in modern society, many people is suffering from anxiety or depression. the biological explain is that your brain does not function well as normal, may cause by stress like family or job issues. Take anxiety for example, there is an receptor called amygdala, which responds to fear. When we are under a highly tense situation our amygdala will be stimulated and raise your negative emotion, which will also slow down our frontal lobe, the brain part that let us making a calm and logical decision. That is what happened in our brain when we suffering from anxiety.

Is there any way to cure anxiety despite medicine therapy? Yes, recent research has prove that by regular practicing meditation, we can effectively control anxiety an help patient maintain stable emotion state and ability to make clear decision. To practice meditation, we first find a quiet and safe place, sit and strengthen our back, and close our eyes. Then keep doing diaphragmatic breathing under the rhythm of below:"three seconds in deep breath, hold it for a second, and then take six seconds out and hold it for a second." By focus on this particular breath style, you brain will switch into a relaxation mode and activate your frontal lobe, and inhibit amygdala, which will make your mind feel more clear and peaceful, which not only useful for patient but normal people.
